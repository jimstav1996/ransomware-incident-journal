## ❓ The 5 W's

### 👤 Who caused the incident?
An organized ransomware threat actor group targeting healthcare organizations.

### 💥 What happened?
Employees opened phishing email attachments that installed malware.  
The attackers deployed ransomware that encrypted critical company files, including patient records.

### ⏰ When did the incident occur?
Tuesday morning at approximately 9:00 a.m.

### 📍 Where did the incident happen?
At a small U.S. healthcare clinic specializing in primary-care services.

### 🎯 Why did the incident happen?
The attackers aimed to extort the organization by encrypting critical systems and demanding payment for the decryption key.

---

## 🧠 Additional Notes
- Critical patient data became unavailable
- Business operations were severely disrupted
- Systems were shut down to contain the incident
- Need to assess backup availability
- Need to review phishing email indicators
- Potential regulatory reporting obligations due to healthcare data
